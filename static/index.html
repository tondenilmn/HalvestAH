PINNACLE SIGNAL GUIDE ▼
TIER 1 LINE MOVEMENT Always ON. Pinnacle moves lines with sharp money only — the strongest signal available.
TIER 1 TL MOVEMENT Always ON. Sharp totals action — highly reliable for any goal-related bet.
TIER 2 HOME / AWAY MOVE Turn ON when Line Movement is STABLE. Odds move without line move means sharp action absorbed via price, not line.
TIER 2 OVER / UNDER MOVE Turn ON when TL Movement is STABLE. Same logic — totals sharp action reflected in odds rather than line.
TIER 3 CLOSING ± Rarely useful. Closing odds are already implied by the AH line. Adds specificity but kills sample size.
